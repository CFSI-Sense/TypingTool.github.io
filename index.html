Federal Survey

Qlik commissioned Market Connections to determine how agencies are managing data and the extent to which they believe data analytics tools can benefit their agency. The blind online survey of 200 respondents included 34% from Defense agencies and 66% from Civilian agencies. All had involvement in the organization's use of data and data analytics, and 20% make the final decision regarding providers offering data analytic tools. Respondent areas of responsibility included agency leadership/executive/command (21%), project/program management (20%) and operations and administration (14%).
